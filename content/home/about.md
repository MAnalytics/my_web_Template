# Biography

Dr Adepeju is a UK-based academic specializing in Geocomputation, Crime and Police data analytics. He has participated in high-impact crime and policing projects, and has extensive experience in collaborating with the law enforcement force, where his work has influenced police practices and helped to reduce crime levels. His current research focus on the development of measures of crime inequalities and risk indicators, taking into account the identified drivers and patterns of crime.
